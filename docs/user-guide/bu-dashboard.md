# Reading your BU / team dashboard

*For BU Leads, Group and Portfolio Leaders, and Executives (and any manager, for their own team). Drafted by HAP-11 (FR-013–019); assembled into the full guide by HAP-21. Keep in step with the in-app help content (FR-072).*

Open **Dashboard** to see your area's AI-maturity rolled up from moderated assessments — you always land on your own scope: a BU Lead sees their BU, a Group or Portfolio Leader their group or portfolio, an Executive all of HIG, and anyone else their own team.

## What the tiles mean

- **Floor level** — the *weakest* level anyone in the group is stuck at, with the honest breakdown ("78% at L1+ · 22% still at L0"). This is deliberately the floor, not an average: a team can average "Level 1" while a quarter of it never left Level 0, and the floor is what tells you that.
- **Mean maturity** — the average across your seven dimensions, with a trend arrow versus last cycle.
- **Per-dimension bars** — one bar per dimension with the value printed, so you can see which capabilities lead and which lag.
- **Completion** — how much of your area has completed (and had moderated) their assessment this cycle.
- **Trend** — how each dimension's mean has moved across closed cycles.

## Why some numbers say "Suppressed" instead of a value

To protect individuals, the platform **never shows an aggregate covering fewer than four people** — and it won't show one that could be *worked out* by subtracting other totals you can see. When a group is too small, its tile reads "Suppressed (group too small)" or "Suppressed (would reveal a smaller group)" rather than a number. This is working as intended: it's how leadership reads real signal without ever being able to infer one person's scores. A group that was large enough when a cycle closed stays visible in history even if people later leave.

## Initiative counts

Tiles that reference AI initiatives show "—" for now; they populate once the initiative register ships.
