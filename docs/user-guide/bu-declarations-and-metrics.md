# Your BU's weekly declaration and monthly metrics

*For BU leads and their delegates. Drafted by HAP-15 (FR-047/048). These are the two things you fill in each cycle that the Harris submission can't derive automatically from the register.*

Open **BU Forms**. You'll see two forms side by side: the **weekly AI-DLC declaration** and the **monthly metrics**. You can fill these in for your own business unit — either as its BU lead, or if you've been granted a delegate role for it.

## The weekly AI-DLC declaration

Each week, declare where you believe your BU sits on the AI-DLC maturity path:

- **Declared level** (0–3) — your assessment of the BU's overall maturity.
- **Next-level target date** — when you expect to reach the next level.
- **RAG** — green/amber/red on your progress.
- An optional **note**.

You submit **one declaration per BU per week**. If you submit again in the same week, it **updates** that week's declaration rather than adding a second one.

### The evidence panel — declared vs measured

Beside the declaration, the **evidence panel** shows what the actual assessment data says, so you can sanity-check your declaration against reality:

- the **measured floor distribution** and **trend** for your BU (drawn from the same rolled-up, privacy-protected figures the dashboards show), and
- the **divergence** between your declared level and the measured floor.

If your BU has fewer than four moderated assessments, the evidence panel shows **"Suppressed"** with no numbers — the same small-population protection applied everywhere, so individual data can never be inferred. Your own declaration history still shows.

> The "measured floor" is the level most of your people actually sit at, taken from each person's **weakest** dimension (the floor rule) — not an average. So if your declaration says level 2 but most people are still weak in at least one dimension, the panel will show that gap. (The exact way this single measured level is summarised is still being finalised for Harris reporting — see the notes for HAP-16.)

## The monthly metrics

Once a month, complete the support and SOR metrics:

- **Year-to-date (YTD) fields** (e.g. customer support volumes) are **pre-filled from last month** so you only adjust the delta — the running total carries forward.
- **Current-month-only fields** — internal support figures and the **SOR "called by other apps"** question start blank each month; they describe this month, not a running total.

Enter this month's numbers and submit. Both forms feed directly into your BU's Harris submission.
